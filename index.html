About Us

Kilkari Play School cum creche

Kilkari Play School cum Creche is a trusted early childhood education and daycare centre located in the heart of Ushanagar, Tezpur, Assam. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, we are dedicated to providing young children with a strong foundation through play-based learning, creative activities, and compassionate care. Our experienced educators ensure that every child feels safe, valued, and excited to learn each day.

At Kilkari, we understand that your child's early years are the most critical for development. That is why we combine structured learning with fun and imaginative play to nurture cognitive, social, and emotional growth. Whether you need a reliable creche during working hours or a stimulating preschool environment for your little one, Kilkari Play School cum Creche is here to support your family with dedication and love.
